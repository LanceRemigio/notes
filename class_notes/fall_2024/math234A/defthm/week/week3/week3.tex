\subsection*{Principal Value of Complex Logarithm}

\begin{ocg}{l1}{l1}{1}
    Given \( z \in \C^{\bullet} \), the unique \( w \in S  \) is called the principal value of the logarithm of \( z  \) and we write \( w = \Log(z) \).
